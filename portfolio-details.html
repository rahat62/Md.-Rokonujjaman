Details About the Project

Student Portal

The Student Interface is designed to provide a smooth and interactive learning experience. Students can easily browse and purchase courses from a wide range of offerings. Once enrolled, they gain access to important learning materials such as course notes, videos, and Q&A services to support their studies. Students can also view their class timetable, ensuring they never miss a live session or exam. Students have the opportunity to take various types of exams: Online MCQ exams can be completed directly on the platform. Online written exams require students to write their answers and upload images of their answer sheets from home. Templated written exams provide a pre-designed template that students must complete within fixed areas and submit for grading. For online classes, students can attend sessions either via Zoom or the platform's interactive class system, where they can engage with teachers in real-time, participate in polls, and answer quizzes directly on their devices. In addition, students can ask questions through the platform’s Q&A service and receive responses from teachers. They also have access to previous class materials to help them catch up on missed content.

Teacher Portal

The Teacher Interface offers a comprehensive set of tools for teachers to manage and interact with their students. Teachers can create and deliver lessons for courses, conduct live sessions, and facilitate interactive online classes. They can engage students with polls and quizzes during these sessions, ensuring active participation. Teachers are responsible for grading students' written exams, which can be either online written exams (where students upload images of their answers) or templated written exams (where students complete fixed-format answer sheets). Teachers review these submissions, assign grades, and provide feedback to help students improve. Teachers can also answer questions posted by students through the Q&A service and review past questions for better guidance. They have access to detailed payment tracking for the services they provide, ensuring they are paid for conducting classes, grading exams, and other tasks. This enables teachers to track their earnings and financial history within the platform.

Administrator Interface

The Administrator Interface is designed for complete control and management of the entire platform. Administrators have access to all features of the system, enabling them to monitor and control the entire workflow between students, teachers, and courses. They ensure the smooth operation of the platform by handling tasks such as user registration, course management, and overseeing the delivery of lessons and exams. Administrators are responsible for managing course creation, student enrollments, and teacher assignments, ensuring that all processes are running efficiently. They can also oversee payments, ensuring that teachers and staff are compensated correctly for their work. This includes tracking payments for courses, exams, and other services. Moreover, administrators handle the security and permissions within the system, controlling user roles and access to various sections of the platform. They also have the ability to generate reports on platform usage, student progress, and financial activity. This allows administrators to manage the system more effectively and make data-driven decisions to improve the platform.
